Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of Artificial Intelligence that enables computers to understand, process, and generate human language. By combining linguistics, computer science, and machine learning, NLP allows machines to analyze text and speech in ways that support meaningful interaction between humans and computers.

Machine learning has significantly advanced NLP by enabling systems to learn patterns directly from data rather than relying solely on manually defined rules. Modern NLP applications use machine learning algorithms to perform tasks such as text classification, sentiment analysis, machine translation, question answering, information extraction, and conversational AI.

Recent developments in deep learning and Large Language Models (LLMs) have further transformed the field, making it possible to build systems that can understand context, generate coherent text, and assist users in various domains, including education, healthcare, business, and research.

This blog explores key concepts, techniques, and practical applications of NLP with machine learning. Through articles, tutorials, and research discussions, I aim to share insights into how language technologies are developed and how they can be applied to solve real-world problems.
