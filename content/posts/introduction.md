Hi, I'm Ika Utami.

I am lecturer and researcher in Artificial Intelligence, Applied Data Science, and Large Language Models (LLMs). 

My work focuses on using AI and data-driven approaches to support learning and improve education. I am especially interested in developing innovative educational systems powered by LLMs and educational data.

This space serves as a platform for sharing my thoughts, research, and experiences in the field of Artificial Intelligence, with a particular focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), and Applied Data Science.

Here, I will regularly post articles, tutorials, lecture notes, and practical insights related to NLP and its applications in education and social science.

I hope the content shared on this website will be useful for students, researchers, educators, and anyone interested in the rapidly evolving world of language AI.
