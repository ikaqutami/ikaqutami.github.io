This page highlights several research projects related to Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Natural Language Processing, and Educational Technology.

## 1. AI-Powered Adaptive English Speaking Tutor
Development of an intelligent tutoring system that utilizes Large Language Models (LLMs) and conversational feedback to improve English speaking proficiency through personalized interactions.

## 2. Automated Educational Assessment using Generative AI
Design and implementation of AI-assisted assessment tools for generating questions, grading responses, and providing formative feedback to students.

## 3. Learning Analytics for Student Performance Prediction
Application of machine learning techniques to analyze learning behavior and predict student academic performance in online learning environments.

## 4. Multimodal AI Agent for Language Learning
Development of an AI agent capable of understanding text, speech, and visual inputs to support adaptive language learning experiences.

## 5. Sentiment Analysis of Student Feedback
Investigation of Natural Language Processing methods for analyzing student opinions and satisfaction from course evaluations and surveys.

## 6. Explainable AI for Educational Decision Support
Research on interpretable machine learning models that provide transparent recommendations for educators and academic administrators.

## 7. Intelligent Chatbot for Academic Advising
Development of a conversational AI system to assist students in obtaining academic information, course recommendations, and administrative guidance.

## 8. Early Warning System for At-Risk Students
Building predictive models to identify students who may experience academic difficulties and require timely intervention.

## 9. NLP-Based Automated Essay Scoring
Exploration of machine learning and deep learning approaches for evaluating student essays and providing constructive writing feedback.

## 10. Educational Knowledge Graph Construction
Development of knowledge graph frameworks that integrate educational resources, learning outcomes, and curriculum structures to support intelligent learning systems.
